## Goal

Working *in* a card: rendered Markdown with editing and checkbox toggling,
screenshots pasted straight from the clipboard, quick idea capture into the
inbox, and triage from inbox onto a milestone.

## Definition of done

- A card can be read, edited, and its acceptance criteria checked off in-app.
- ⌘V with an image on the clipboard attaches it to the card
  (`.gello/assets/<card-id>/`), inserts the link, and renders it inline.
- A new idea reaches the inbox in under 5 seconds from app focus.
- Triage moves the card file and rewrites its relative asset links.
